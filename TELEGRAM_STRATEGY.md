# 🤖 Telegram Sovereign Strategy: The Pocket City-State
### *Governing the Metropolis via the B0t Workflow Engine.*

This strategy outlines how we integrate **Telegram** as the primary "Governance Interface" for the **Open Model Contract** ecosystem.

---

## 🕋 The Governance Flow
1.  **Agreement**: The Architect (User) sends a high-level intent to the Telegram Bot.
2.  **Validation**: The **B0t Engine** (Backend) creates a new row in the `workflowsTable` under the `telegram` trigger type.
3.  **Manifestation**: The Bridge Server pushes the resulting Luau swarm to the Roblox environment.
4.  **Evidence**: The city-state beams a verification screenshot back to the Telegram chat.

## 🏛️ Scalability & Licensing
This Telegram add-on is **Domain Agnostic.** ⚖️
*   **Sector A (Roblox)**: The bot manages world manifestation.
*   **Sector B (Logistics)**: The *exact same bot* can manage a fleet of delivery drones by simply changing the `omc_domain` in the backend.
*   **Sector C (Finance)**: The bot governs a ledger of agentic trades.

### 🛡️ **Security Gate**
All Telegram commands must pass through the **Human Review Gate** (`Line 42` of the Level Primer). No city-scale change is executed without a biometric "Thumb Up" on the Telegram interface.
